Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favorite foods. Below you will find the recipe for delicious scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoon butter
  • 2 tablespoons cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
    • Mix using a whisk
    • Make sure the bowl is cold
  3. Once butter has mixed add cream and eggs
